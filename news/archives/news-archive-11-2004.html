EX Team Rocket Returns update
According to Pokémon-TCG.com, the EX Team Rocket Returns expansion will be released on Monday, and there is some more info on the site. It will have more than 100 new cards, new Pokémon-EX, new Trainer cards, and a new level of rare card (Pokémon "star"). Dark and Rocket's Pokémon will also reappear in this set. The Pokémon "star" have a new foil treatment and artwork, and introduce some new abilities to the game, but it sounds like they'll be comparable in rarity to the "Shining" Pokémon from Neo Destiny.

Starting Monday, booster packs containing 1 reverse holofoil, 1 rare, 2 uncommon, and 5 common cards will sell for MSRP $3.29; and 60-card theme decks including an updated rulebook, Team Rocket-themed game coin, half-playmat, one holofoil card, and a card checklist will sell for MSRP $10.99.

Speaking of Pokémon-TCG.com, their PDF-format checklists haven't been working lately, so I'll have to work with the limited information available from the paper checklist from my brother's Team Aqua deck.
