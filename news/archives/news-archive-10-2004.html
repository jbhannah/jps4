CotW Week 2, Deoxys at Pokémon Center NYC, and TCG news
That's right, the second Card of the Week is up today, and this time it's Team Aqua's Walrein from the EX Team Aqua vs. Team Magma set. (This has got to be one of my favorite cards ever made. Read the review for more.) The Team Aqua deck will also come under the microscope sometime next week in the Deck Garage, so watch for that.

The Pokémon Center NYC is having a Halloween party this Saturday. There will be Pokémon costume contests, music & games, and Pokémon Mini tournaments. Also, trainers with FireRed or LeafGreen can get the Aurora Ticket from this event, which is needed to catch Deoxys. (See the Pokémon Rocks America 2004 article below.)

In the TCG world, two new products are available: the EX Battle Stadium pack, which has 2 60-card theme decks, 4 10-card sideboard packs, a full-size playmat, and more; and EX Deck Tins, which come in 4 designs and include 2 EX series booster packs, a holofoil card, and a Pokémon sticker pack. Coming soon, there will be Pokémon TCG: EX Team Rocket Returns Prerelease Tournaments being held presumably nationwide, and each participant gets a free EX Team Rocket Returns booster. (The link is the one given on http://www.op.pokemon-tcg.com/, but at the time I posted this it didn't work. More info when the page does work.)

Pokémon Rocks America 2004 in Seattle
The Pokémon Rocks America 2004 tour stopped in Seattle, Washington today, and once again Deoxys (a new "secret" Pokémon in FireRed/LeafGreen) was the main attraction in the exhibition hall. As usual, there were attractions such as costumed characters, TCG and video game booths, tournaments, and carnival games; new with the 2004 tour was the Union Room, where attendees with Pokémon FireRed or LeafGreen could battle and trade wirelessly via the adapter included with the game. On the original article page (linked below), there are Flash applets with photo slideshows and videos from the event. There wasn't an end-of-day recap available as of this update, but I'll post one tomorrow.
